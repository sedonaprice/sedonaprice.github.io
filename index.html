I am currently a postdoctoral scholar in the IR/Sub-MM group at the Max Planck Institute for Extraterrestrial Physics.

I completed my PhD in the Astronomy Department at UC Berkeley, working with Mariska Kriek. I have worked with the MOSDEF, 3D-HST, and MassiveFIRE collaborations to study star-forming high redshift galaxies.

Prior to my time at Berkeley, I did my undergraduate studies in Physics at Caltech.
